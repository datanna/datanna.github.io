Does Online Dating Work? 8 People on Finding Love on the Internet

We decided to bring the question to real people who have navigated dating apps to find out what it's really like.Read on to learn their thoughts on how well online dating really works. The search for love in the digital age tends to stir up a lot of anxiety. If you've ever heard stories from your friends' bad dates, you might reasonably approach dating apps with caution. Many of us know couples who seem so perfectly matched that it's almost impossible to believe they met on opposite sides of a screen.When it comes down to it, does online dating actually work? While you might be worried it's not a good idea (or even a waste of time), like all matters in love, it has its pros and cons. So let's put the fears to rest—and put the internet to the test. But just as online dating can foster some comically-bad experiences, there are plenty of benefits as well.
